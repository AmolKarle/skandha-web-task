About Skandha Media Services

Skandha Media Services is the premium Global Video Service provider for DigitalTV and PayTV Operators enabling our customer to implement the cloud video & content strategy for digital business. Skandha Media Services through its Digital Operation Service facility in APAC and MEA region offers some of the most innovative solution for the operators including Content owners, Aggregators, Broadcasting, MSO's & Telecom providers. The key offering includes Content Syndication, Video IAAS, Digital Operation Management, Advisory and System Integration. At Skandha, we enable service providers (Broadcasters, OTT, Cable, Telecom & other content owners) to migrate and capitalize their Video business by moving to cloud through our opex/offshore model whereby Skandha manage and deliver the digital service on-behalf of customer. We manage the Technology and Service SLA that is offered to end-subscribers. The main focus of Skandha is toward Quality of User Experience, Revenue generation & Growth for all the stakeholders including partners and customers.
